Our History

The Food Company" founded in 1997. Nathalie & Vanessa has been best friend since childhood, so they had a dream of opening their own restaurant as they are super foodie and like the way a chef decorate the plat and present it to clients. It is a fascinating world which demands a lot of hard work and patience. Price is just a number. Service and quality should be on the top which makes "The Food Company" one of the best corner in the region to have a brunch. Nathalie always wanted to open a cafeteria, so we mix up the ideas of Nathalie & Vanessa to get what it is today. Thank you.
